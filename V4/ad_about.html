ABOUT: Admin_Tools

Admin_Tools, is a place that I feel may be useful to future IT Technicians. It contains Links to Administrative tools that has helped me along my IT path. I wanted to create a place, where the tools that I use can be easily accessed from anywhere, anytime. As I gain the experience of uing more tools I will be upading this site with the tools that I come across in the coming future. I hope that it can help you out as well. I have created a "Comments" area for you administrators to leave a link to the tools they have also used. so please feel free to leave a link. I will go through the links suggested, for system compatibility and functionality.
